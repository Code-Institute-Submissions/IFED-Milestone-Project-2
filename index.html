Instructions:

Pokemon Match Up is a matching pairs memory game. Choose your difficulty to start a game. The timer begins when you click a tile. To beat the game, you must match 2 of each tile with the same Pokemon, until there are no tiles remaining. Aim to be the fastest on the leaderboard! To reset the game, press the 'Reset' button.
